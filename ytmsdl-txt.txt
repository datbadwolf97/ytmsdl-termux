Welcome to YTMSDL (Youtube Music Downloader)

Make sure that you've installed the dependencies.
Here's the dependencies :
ffmpeg
python (pip youtube-dl)
figlet

>>> Quickstart :
1.	Tap "Share" button on Youtube or Youtube Music app then tap "Copy to clipboard".
2.	Type the considered formats below alongside with space, then paste the link, press ENTER to start downloading.

Formats available :

m4a	(Recomended)
mp3	(Best for compability)
flac	(Not recomended)
wav	(Best for audio editing)
ogg	(Best for ringtone)
aac	(Optional)
wma	(Optional)
